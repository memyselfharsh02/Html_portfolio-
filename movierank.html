My top 3 Movie of all time

Top Gun

"Top Gun" is a 1986 American action film directed by Tony Scott. The movie follows a talented fighter pilot named Maverick, played by Tom Cruise, as he attends the elite Top Gun Naval Fighter Weapons School, where he competes to become the best pilot while dealing with personal challenges and intense aerial dogfights. The film is known for its adrenaline-pumping flight sequences and iconic soundtrack, becoming a classic in the action genre.

Iron Man

Iron Man, portrayed by Tony Stark, is a brilliant billionaire inventor and a charismatic superhero in the Marvel universe. Donning a high-tech suit of armor he designed himself, he uses his engineering prowess and resources to fight crime and protect the world, while his quick wit and ego add complexity to his character.

Whiplash

"Whiplash" is an intense drama film directed by Damien Chazelle. It follows the ambitious young drummer Andrew Neiman as he enrolls in a prestigious music conservatory and enters into a tumultuous mentorship with his demanding and abusive instructor, Terence Fletcher. The movie explores themes of obsession, sacrifice , and the pursuit of greatness through a gripping narrative centered around the competitive world of jazz music.
